Executive Summary (Plain Language)

FilePino has strong content volume and broad indexability, but operational risk remains high due to accessibility gaps, performance bottlenecks, and trust-signal deficits.

What is working well

  • Cross-browser behavior and baseline responsiveness are mostly stable in manual checks.
  • Core SEO, crawlability, and URL structure are comparatively strong.
  • Most major navigation paths are available and indexable.

What needs immediate action

  • Accessibility defects and missing page landmarks create user and compliance risk.
  • Slow server response and heavy pages impact conversion and retention.
  • Missing About/Contact/Privacy/author/date trust signals reduce credibility.

Priority Findings

User Experience

  • Near-tablet responsiveness issues and mobile dropdown behavior remain problematic.
  • Download button and mobile WhatsApp footer action are not reliable.
  • Phone input validation accepts invalid characters.

Technical Debt

  • Large warning volume indicates unresolved structural issues across many templates.
  • Broken external links and orphan-link patterns reduce quality and crawl efficiency.
  • Legacy/outdated pages (for example compliance-calendar) need governance decisions.

Performance Opportunities

  • Server response times are frequently in slow or very slow ranges (~900ms to 1.3s).
  • Image optimization, lazy loading, caching, and JS/CSS optimization are significant quick wins.
  • High page weight increases abandonment risk for mobile users.

Governance & Compliance

  • Accessibility backlog is substantial and should be treated as a formal remediation track.
  • Privacy and contact/legal trust pages are missing in E-E-A-T checks.
  • WordPress/plugin update discipline and legal-page ownership should be explicit.

Action Plan by Timeline

Immediate (0-7 days)

  • Fix top accessibility blockers: empty links/buttons, form labels, missing main landmarks.
  • Repair broken UX actions: mobile dropdown behavior, download button, WhatsApp footer link.
  • Triage and remove outdated/low-value pages from active navigation if needed.

Near-Term (1-4 weeks)

  • Enable caching and compression; prioritize image and script optimization.
  • Add structured data basics and repair critical internal/external link quality issues.
  • Implement proper phone validation and full form QA coverage.

Ongoing Maintenance

  • Adopt monthly accessibility and performance checkpoint audits.
  • Track score trends after each release and maintain regression checklist.
  • Maintain legal/trust pages and author/date metadata as standard editorial policy.

Priority Technical Items

  • https://www.filepino.com/compliance-calendar/ (review for outdated content)
  • Download button behavior in blog/service pages (functional bug)
  • Mobile footer WhatsApp action (broken on mobile tests)
  • E-E-A-T trust pages: /about, /contact, /privacy-policy (currently missing)
