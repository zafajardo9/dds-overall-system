# Site Audit Report & Presentation Framework

## 1. Executive Summary (The "So What?")

_Start here for stakeholders and decision-makers who need the "big picture" before diving into technicalities._

- **Overall Health Score:** A numerical grade or percentage (e.g., 82/100).
- **The "Three Pillar" Summary:**
  - **The Good:** What is currently working (e.g., "Strong backlink profile").
  - **The Bad:** The most critical blockers (e.g., "Massive crawl errors").
  - **The Opportunity:** Estimated traffic or revenue lift if fixes are implemented.
- **Top 3 High-Impact Wins:** The first things the team should tackle tomorrow.

---

## 2. Technical Infrastructure (The Foundation)

_Focus on how search engines and bots interact with the site._

- **Crawlability & Indexing:** \* Review of `robots.txt` and XML Sitemaps.
  - Identification of 404 errors, 5xx server errors, and redirect loops.
- **Site Architecture:**
  - Evaluation of URL structure and internal linking depth.
- **Security:** \* HTTPS status and SSL certificate validity.

---

## 3. Performance & Page Experience (The Speed)

_Focus on Core Web Vitals and user-centric loading metrics._

- **Core Web Vitals (CWV):** \* LCP (Loading), FID/INP (Interactivity), and CLS (Visual Stability).
- **Page Speed:** \* Mobile vs. Desktop performance scores.
- **Mobile-Friendliness:** \* Responsive design check and viewport issues.

---

## 4. On-Page & Content Analysis (The Substance)

_Reviewing the actual pages and how they are optimized for keywords._

- **Metadata Audit:** \* Missing or duplicate Title tags and Meta descriptions.
- **Header Hierarchy:** \* Correct usage of H1-H6 tags.
  - **Keyword Alignment:** Are pages targeting the right search intent?
- **Content Quality:** \* Identification of "thin" content or duplicate content (cannibalization).
- **Image Optimization:** \* Missing Alt text and uncompressed file sizes.

---

## 5. User Experience (UX) & Conversion (The Flow)

_Where SEO meets the human user._

- **Navigation & Menu:** Is the "Path to Purchase" clear?
- **Call to Action (CTA) Effectiveness:** Are buttons visible, clickable, and compelling?
- **Accessibility (WCAG):** Color contrast, screen reader compatibility, and font legibility.

---

## 6. Competitive Benchmarking

_How the site stacks up against the "other guys."_

- **Gap Analysis:** Keywords the competitors rank for that you don't.
- **Backlink Comparison:** Domain Authority vs. top 3 competitors.
- **Speed Benchmarking:** A side-by-side speed comparison.

---

## 7. The Prioritization Matrix (The Roadmap)

_Crucial for the "Presentation" phase to prevent overwhelm._

| Priority          | Task Description                        | Business Impact | Dev Effort |
| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------- | :-------------- | :--------- |
| **P0 (Critical)** | Fix 404 redirects on high-traffic pages | High            | Low        |
| **P1 (High)**     | Compress site-wide images for speed     | Medium          | Low        |
| **P2 (Medium)**   | Restructure Blog Categories             | High            | High       |
| **P3 (Low)**      | Update Alt-text for decorative images   | Low             | Low        |

---

## 8. Next Steps & Timeline

- **Week 1-2:** Technical "Quick Wins" and Critical Fixes.
- **Week 3-4:** Content Optimization and UX Tweaks.
- **Month 2:** Long-term architectural changes and monitoring.
